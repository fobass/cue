# Privacy Policy for Cue

**Last Updated: January 21, 2026**

## Introduction

Welcome to Cue ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Cue (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information You Provide

**Voice Recordings**
- We temporarily process voice recordings to create reminders via speech recognition
- Voice recordings are processed locally on your device and immediately converted to text
- We do not store raw audio files
- Transcribed text is sent to our backend AI service to parse reminder details
- Voice data is deleted immediately after transcription

**Reminders**
- Reminder text content
- Dates and times for scheduled reminders
- Location names and coordinates (if you enable location-based reminders)
- Recurrence patterns
- Original voice transcripts (stored locally on your device)

**Account Information**
- Apple ID authentication token (via Sign in with Apple)
- No email addresses or personal identifiers are collected beyond what Apple provides

### 2. Information Collected Automatically

**Location Information**
- When you create location-based reminders (Premium feature), we collect:
  - Your current location coordinates
  - Locations you specify for reminders
  - Geofence monitoring data
- Location data is used solely to trigger reminders when you arrive at specified places
- Location tracking only occurs when you have active location-based reminders
- You can control location permissions through your device settings

**Device Information**
- Device type and model
- Operating system version
- App version
- Device identifiers for subscription management
- Language and timezone settings

**Usage Information**
- App interactions and feature usage
- Crash reports and error logs
- Performance metrics

### 3. Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do NOT record or store your voice beyond the immediate transcription
- We do NOT access your contacts, photos, or other personal files
- We do NOT track your location continuously (only when you have active location reminders)
- We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties

## How We Use Your Information

### To Provide Core Services
- Convert your voice to text for reminder creation
- Parse and understand reminder details (date, time, location, recurrence)
- Schedule and deliver notifications
- Trigger location-based reminders when you arrive at places
- Sync reminders across your devices (if using iCloud)

### To Improve Our Services
- Analyze usage patterns to improve AI accuracy
- Fix bugs and improve app performance
- Develop new features
- Monitor app stability

### To Manage Subscriptions
- Process premium subscription purchases
- Manage subscription status and access to premium features
- Send subscription-related notifications
- Handle refunds and billing inquiries

### Legal Compliance
- Comply with applicable laws and regulations
- Respond to legal requests and prevent fraud
- Enforce our Terms of Service

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- All reminders are stored locally on your device
- Voice transcripts are stored in local app storage
- Location data for reminders is stored locally

### Cloud Storage (Optional)
- If you enable iCloud sync, reminder data is stored in your private iCloud account
- We do not have access to your iCloud data
- Apple's iCloud privacy policy applies

### Backend Processing
- Voice transcripts are sent to our secure backend AI service for parsing
- Transcripts are processed in real-time and not permanently stored
- All communications are encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
- Backend servers are located in secure data centers

### Security Measures
- End-to-end encryption for data transmission
- Secure authentication via Sign in with Apple
- Regular security audits and updates
- Access controls and monitoring
- Data is never sold or shared with third parties

## Location Services

### How We Use Location
- **Background Location Tracking**: Used only when you have active location-based reminders
- **Geofencing**: We monitor specific locations you set for reminders
- **Smart Location Search**: We search for places near you when you create location reminders

### Your Control
- You can enable/disable location services at any time in Settings
- You can choose "When In Use" or "Always" location permission
- You can delete location-based reminders to stop location tracking
- Location data is only used for reminder functionality

### Location Privacy
- We do not track your location history
- We do not share your location with third parties
- Location data is not used for advertising
- You can revoke location permission at any time

## Third-Party Services

### Apple Services
- **Sign in with Apple**: Authentication service (Apple's privacy policy applies)
- **StoreKit**: In-app purchase processing (Apple's privacy policy applies)
- **CloudKit**: Optional iCloud sync (Apple's privacy policy applies)
- **Core Location**: Location services (controlled by iOS permissions)
- **Speech Recognition**: On-device speech-to-text (data stays on device)

### AI Backend Service
- We use a secure backend AI service to parse reminder details
- Only transcribed text is sent (never audio recordings)
- Data is processed in real-time and not stored permanently
- Service is hosted on DigitalOcean with enterprise-grade security

### Analytics (Optional)
- We may use privacy-focused analytics to improve the app
- All analytics data is anonymized
- You can opt out of analytics in Settings

## Children's Privacy

Cue is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will delete such information.

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access and Control
- **View Your Data**: Access all your reminders in the app
- **Delete Your Data**: Delete individual reminders or clear all data in Settings
- **Export Your Data**: Request a copy of your data by contacting us
- **Delete Your Account**: Sign out and delete the app to remove all local data

### Communication Preferences
- **Notifications**: Control notification settings in iOS Settings
- **Marketing**: We do not send marketing emails or messages

### Location Permissions
- **Change Permissions**: Modify location access in iOS Settings > Cue
- **Revoke Access**: Disable location services at any time

## Data Retention

- **Active Reminders**: Stored until you delete them
- **Completed Reminders**: Automatically archived (can be deleted)
- **Voice Transcripts**: Stored locally on your device until you delete them
- **Backend Processing**: Transcript data is immediately discarded after parsing
- **Account Deletion**: All data is deleted when you delete the app
